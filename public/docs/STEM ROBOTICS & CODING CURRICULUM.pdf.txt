ABOUT
COMPANY
Founded in 2016, PNT Academy has earned national
recognition for its impactful work in robotics education
and hands-on training. While our primary focus is on
student-centric,  project-based  learning,  our  sister
company, PNT Robotics, has been appreciated by Hon’ble
Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi and featured on Shark
Tank India, where it secured investment from Peyush
Bansal.
We specialize in delivering engaging, practical learning
experiences,  and  have  partnered  with  prestigious
institutions such as the Indian Navy, DRDO, and TATA Power.
These collaborations enable us to offer students real-world
exposure through internships, workshops, and industrial
training programs.
At PNT Academy, our mission is to bridge the gap between
classroom learning and real-world industry demands—
empowering students with the future-ready skills needed
to innovate, build, and lead in tomorrow’s world.

PROPOSAL
We proudly present the STEM Robotics & Coding Curriculum —  a
year-long program designed to equip students with 21st-century
skills in robotics, programming, and hands-on engineering.
Implemented across leading schools in India, the program offers a
structured journey: starting with fundamentals of robotics and
electronics, progressing to coding in block-based and Python
languages, and advancing into AI, IoT, and automation projects
tailored to each grade level.
More than just a curriculum, this is a practical learning platform
that transforms classrooms into innovation hubs —  preparing
students to think critically, solve problems creatively, and compete
confidently at district, national, and global levels.
