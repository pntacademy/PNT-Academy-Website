ABOUT US
 
Founded in 2016, PNT Academy has earned national
recognition for its impactful work in robotics education
and hands-on training. While our primary focus is on
student-centric, project-based learning, our sister
company, PNT Robotics, has been appreciated by Hon’ble
Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi and featured on Shark
Tank India, where it secured investment from Peyush
Bansal.
We specialize in delivering engaging, practical learning
experiences, and have partnered with prestigious
institutions such as the Indian Navy, DRDO, and TATA
Power. These collaborations enable us to offer students
real-world exposure through internships, workshops, and
industrial training programs.
At PNT Academy, our mission is to bridge the gap
between classroom learning and real-world industry
demands—empowering students with the future-ready
skills needed to innovate, build, and lead in tomorrow’s
world.

Proposal
 
We offer a hands-on Robotics Workshop Program
designed to introduce students to practical technology
learning, problem-solving, and innovation.
Schools can choose one topic from 15+ engaging
robotics modules, and the selected workshop is
conducted through guided, activity-based sessions.
As an extended opportunity, students may also
participate in an online internship based on the Indian
Navy’s Project ‘Kavach’, gaining exposure to applied
engineering concepts under expert mentorship.
Students further get the opportunity to showcase their
learning through Skill Tank 2026–27, PNT Academy’s in-
house Robotics & Innovation competition, along with
exposure to national-level student technology events,
including tech festivals hosted by institutes such as IIT
Bombay.
